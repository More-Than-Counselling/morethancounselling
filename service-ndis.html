01

Therapeutic Counselling

Capacity Building

Improved Daily Living

Living with a disability or chronic condition can bring unexpected emotional challenges … grief, frustration, identity shifts, and the weight of adjusting to a life that may look very different than you’d planned.

Therapeutic counselling provides a supported space to process those experiences, develop coping strategies, and build the psychological skills and resilience to navigate daily life with greater clarity and confidence.

Sessions are integrative, trauma-informed, and tailored to your individual goals and NDIS plan.

02

Movement-Based Capacity Building

Capacity Building

Improved Health & Wellbeing  /  Improved Daily Living

For participants with physical restrictions or mobility challenges, I offer therapeutic movement support designed to help you safely increase your functional capacity, body confidence, and independence in daily life.

As a qualified counsellor and certified yoga teacher with access to specialist movement equipment, I draw on evidence-informed therapeutic movement principles to support you in:

  • Improving balance, coordination, and safe body movement
  • Building strength and mobility to support everyday activities
  • Developing body awareness and confidence in physical function
  • Reducing barriers to participation in daily and community life

This support is delivered at a pace that respects your physical needs and comfort … and is always grounded in a trauma-informed, therapeutic framework that considers the whole person, not just the physical.

In-person Self & plan-managed CB: Improved Health & Wellbeing CB: Improved Daily Living

03

Support Worker Services

Core Supports

Assistance with Daily Life

I offer personalised support worker services for participants who need practical, compassionate assistance with daily life activities.

As a qualified therapeutic practitioner with First Aid certification and a current NDIS Worker Screening Clearance, I bring a considered, person-centred approach to support work … one that goes beyond task assistance to help you build confidence, independence, and capacity in your everyday routines.

Support may include assistance with daily activities, community access, and working toward the goals in your NDIS plan in a way that feels respectful and meaningful to you.
