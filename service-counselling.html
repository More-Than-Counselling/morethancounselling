One-on-one support

Individual Counselling

This is not just talk therapy. It is a space where your whole experience is understood ... your body, your history, your nervous system, and the life you are trying to rebuild. Sessions are tailored, evidence-based, and grounded in genuine care.

Book a Session

Who it’s for

This may be for you if...

  • You’ve received a diagnosis and don’t know who you are anymore
  • You’re managing chronic illness or pain
  • You’re experiencing anxiety, burnout, or emotional exhaustion
  • You’ve been through grief, loss, or significant life change
  • You feel stuck and don’t know why
  • You want support that understands the mind-body connection
  • You’re an NDIS participant needing therapeutic support
  • You simply feel like yourself has disappeared

What to expect

Your sessions, explained

01

First Session

We take time to understand your story and what you most need. There's no pressure to have answers ... we find the direction together.

02

The Work

Sessions draw on evidence-based approaches including CBT, ACT, trauma-informed practice, and nervous system regulation. We go at your pace.

03

Between Sessions

You may leave with a small practice, a reflection, or simply more clarity. The work continues between sessions in everyday life.

04

Over Time

Progress isn't always linear, but it is real. Many clients notice shifts in how they relate to themselves, their body, and what feels possible.

The approach

Evidence-based and whole-person

Fionna draws on Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), trauma-informed practice, nervous system regulation, and somatic awareness. Sessions are never one-size-fits-all ... what you need shapes how we work.

The focus is always on what is most meaningful to you right now. Some clients need practical tools. Others need a space to be witnessed and understood. Most need both.
