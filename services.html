What I Offer

Support that meets you
where you are

Whether you've just received a diagnosis, are navigating chronic illness, burnout, or simply feel like the person you used to be has disappeared... there is a path forward. Support here is not about fixing what's wrong with you. It's about understanding what you've been carrying, and finding a way through it. Services are available privately and through the NDIS.

01

Individual Counselling

One-on-one sessions creating genuine safety to process what you're carrying. Drawing on evidence-based counselling, neuroscience, and an understanding of how the nervous system and physical health interact — we work through grief, identity shifts, anxiety, and the practical reality of what comes next.

Learn More →
02

Yoga & Movement Sessions

Gentle, therapeutic yoga designed for bodies that have been through something. Whether you're post-surgery, managing a chronic condition, or rebuilding trust in movement after a long period of stress — sessions meet you where you are. We work with what your body can do, not against what it can't.

Learn More →
03

Breathwork & Meditation

Science-backed breathwork and mindfulness practices that work directly with your nervous system to reduce anxiety, improve sleep, and restore a genuine sense of calm. Available standalone or woven naturally into your counselling work.

Learn More →
04

NDIS Therapeutic Support

Therapeutic support and capacity building for NDIS participants. Working within your plan to build emotional regulation, daily living skills, and long-term wellbeing — support that meets you where you are and grows with you over time.

Learn More →
05

Workshops & Online Courses

Group workshops and self-paced online courses bringing together counselling, nervous system education, movement, and practical wellbeing tools — for people who want to understand themselves more deeply.

Learn More →
