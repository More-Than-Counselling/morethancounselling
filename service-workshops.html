Workshops & Online Courses

Group workshops and self-paced online courses bringing together counselling, nervous system education, therapeutic movement, and practical wellbeing tools — for people who want to understand themselves more deeply and build skills they can use every day.

Who it’s for

This may be for you if...

  • You want to understand how your nervous system works
  • You’re looking for practical tools to manage anxiety and stress
  • You want to learn alongside others on a similar path
  • One-on-one sessions aren’t the right fit right now
  • You want to learn at your own pace, in your own time

Your sessions, explained

All content draws on evidence-based counselling, neuroscience, therapeutic yoga, and breathwork — made accessible and genuinely useful.

04

Workshop participants often describe the shared experience of being understood as one of the most valuable parts. You are not alone.

The approach

Education that creates real change

These offerings are built on the belief that understanding yourself is one of the most powerful things you can do. When you understand why your nervous system responds the way it does, you can start to make different choices.

Content is grounded in neuroscience, counselling theory, and Fionna’s clinical and lived experience.
